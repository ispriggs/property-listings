Our Mission

Conscious community living in Costa Rica

The Machuca Valley is more than a place to live, it's a way of life. Nestled in the heart of Costa Rica's Central Pacific region, our communities are built on the principles of ecological harmony, intentional living, and genuine human connection.

From forest casitas to solar-powered villas, every listing in our portfolio reflects our commitment to sustainable living without sacrificing comfort or beauty.
