Search by Community

Each community offers a distinct way of living — from cloud forest retreats to vibrant village life.

La Ecovilla (LEV)

A community nestled in the valley, surrounded by a permaculture jungle. Just 10 minutes from San Mateo and 18 minutes from Orotina.

Alegría Village

A newer community still carving its path. Perched higher up the hill, it enjoys a cooler climate and spectacular views. Located 20 minutes from San Mateo and 30 minutes from Orotina.

La Ecovilla San Mateo (ESM)

The newest and largest of the communities, featuring multiple shared community spaces. Only 6 minutes from San Mateo and 16 minutes from Orotina.

Tacotal

The oldest of all the communities. More rooted in the valley and primarily off-grid, offering a deeper connection to nature. Located 13 minutes from San Mateo and 23 minutes from Orotina.

Our Mission

Conscious community living in Costa Rica

The Machuca Valley is more than a place to live — it's a way of life. Nestled in the heart of Costa Rica's Central Pacific region, our communities are built on the principles of ecological harmony, intentional living, and genuine human connection.

From cloud forest casitas to solar-powered villas, every listing in our portfolio reflects our commitment to sustainable living without sacrificing comfort or beauty.
